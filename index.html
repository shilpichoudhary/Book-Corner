Popular Books

The Immortals Of Meluha

The story is set in the land of Meluha and starts with the arrival of the Shiva. The Meluhans believe that Shiva is their fabled saviour Neelkanth.

Chitralekha

Chitralekha is a 1934 Hindi novel by the Indian novel writer Bhagwati Charan Verma about the philosophy of life, love, sin and virtue. The must read book.

Lost in Dreams

This historical novel has created history in the world of literature and books. This is a biographical novel on Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. He is the most worshipped person in India.

Indians

In this riveting book, Namit Arora takes us on an unforgettable journey through 5000 years of history, reimagining in rich detail. Know the truth.

Ram - Scion of Ikshvaku

Amish's Ram Chandra Series reimagines Ramayana in 3400 BCE, where Prince Ram rises from torment to lead a divided Ayodhya. With Sita and his brothers, he battles Raavan's tyranny and chaos. A tale of love, resilience, and destiny unfolds in this epic saga.

Kai Chand The Sare Aasman

This book captures the Indo-Islamic culture and literary society of 18th-19th century Delhi, tracing shifts from Rajputana to the Red Fort. It explores Hindustani artistry, British influence, and vibrant figures like Ghalib and Bahadur Shah Zafar.

War Of Lanka

In the epic fourth book of the *Ram Chandra Series*, Ram, Sita, and Raavan’s journeys collide in a devastating war for Dharma. As Sita is kidnapped and Ram prepares for battle, the ruthless Raavan faces a force beyond his reckoning. A clash of fury, love, and destiny.

Raavan - Enemy of Aryavarta

In the third book of the *Ram Chandra Series*, Raavan’s journey unfolds, revealing a complex man torn between love, violence, and destiny. A formidable pirate and king, he seeks greatness while grappling with fate's cruel tests.
